Services

We provide all scheduled mileage services

including 30,000, 60,000 and 90,000 mile services.

Any shop that does not look for potential problems is actually doing you a great disservice!

Something as simple as discovering and changing a worn belt can save you potential damage to the vehicle not to mention an inconvenient or dangerous brake down.

90% of all vehicles we service receive a road test and a visual safety check at no charge. Any recommendations are then prepared as prioritized estimates. This gives you the information you need to make educated decisions about your cars care. This can be very helpful when deciding if your car should be repaired or replaced

We have all manufacturer's maintenance schedules on our computer system. We will be happy to provide a printed maintenance schedule specific to your vehicle and driving style. These services are essential to maximize the life and reliability of your investment.

We offer a thorough vehicle inspection package (not state inspection). Including checking the mechanical condition of the vehicle, all maintenance items and fluids as well as checking for body and frame rot. This is ideal for winterization, a used car pre-purchase check or before a road trip.
